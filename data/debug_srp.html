Chennai to Bangalore Bus Overview

The Chennai to Bangalore bus route covers approximately 332 kms and takes about 7 hrs 4 mins. A total of 223 direct buses operated by 72 bus operators run on this route. The first bus departs at 00:30, and the last bus leaves at 23:59. 

Major boarding points in Chennai include Adyar, Alandur, Ambattur, Aminjikarai, Anna Nagar, Anna University, Arumbakkam, Ashok Pillar, Avadi, Central Railway Station, while common drop locations in Bangalore are Anand Rao Circle, Attibele, Baiyappanahalli, Banashankari, Banaswadi, Battarahalli, Bellandur, Bommanahalli, Bommasandra, Byatarayanapura.

RTC and private operators such as IntrCity SmartBus, National Travels NTA, NueGo, SPS Luxury Travels, Jabbar Travels offer Volvo, AC, and non-AC seater and sleeper buses. Ticket prices on redBus range from INR 400 - INR 3999.00 depending on the bus type and features.
